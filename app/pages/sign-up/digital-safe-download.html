Download my digital safe

To provide secure and private access to your online accounts, Wealth Hub uses a digital safe. The safe is stored on your device and it's where your usernames and passwords are encrypted. When you access your online accounts through Wealth Hub, your login information travels between your digital safe and that account provider. Your login credentials are never seen, collected, or stored by us and are never disclosed to any third party. When you log off from Wealth Hub, your digital safe is locked ensuring your details are kept secure.
